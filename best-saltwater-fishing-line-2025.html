💡 Pro Tips for Saltwater Line Success

🧂 Saltwater Line Maintenance

  • Rinse after every trip: Salt crystals cut line over time
  • Check for nicks: Inspect first 20 feet regularly
  • Retie frequently: Saltwater weakens knots faster
  • Store properly: Keep out of UV light when not fishing

🎣 Species-Specific Recommendations

  • Tarpon: 30-50lb mono for stretch and shock absorption
  • Snook: 15-20lb fluorocarbon for clear water stealth
  • Redfish: 20-25lb braid to fluoro leader combo
  • Tuna: 50-80lb mono or wire for speed trolling
  • Sharks: Always use heavy wire leader (100lb+)

🌊 Seasonal Considerations

  • Spring: Clear water = lighter, less visible lines
  • Summer: Structure fishing = heavier, abrasion-resistant lines
  • Fall: Bait runs = medium weight for versatility
  • Winter: Deep water = thin diameter, high strength braids
